# Bargains with Brooke — Holiday Content Calendar & Research

Based on what's performed well on top deal blogs (DealNews, Good Housekeeping, Forbes Vetted, Tom's Guide) and current TikTok/social trends for 2026.

---

## Upcoming Holidays (May–September 2026)

### 1. MEMORIAL DAY (May 25, 2026)
**Publish window:** May 18–25
**What performs best on deal blogs:**
- Outdoor/patio furniture roundups (Wayfair, Target, Walmart — up to 60-75% off)
- Mattress deal guides (historically the #1 Memorial Day affiliate category)
- Grill & BBQ essentials (Blackstone, portable grills, grill accessories)
- Summer wardrobe kickoff (swimwear, shorts, sandals)
- Patriotic home decor (red/white/blue entertaining)

**Blog post ideas:**
- "memorial day outdoor deals you don't want to miss" — patio sets, string lights, outdoor rugs from Target + Walmart
- "best grill accessories under $25" — tongs, thermometers, grill mats, aprons
- "summer entertaining on a budget" — serving trays, outdoor plates, coolers, drink dispensers
- "backyard upgrade under $100" — Target+Walmart outdoor finds (bistro sets, planters, solar lights)

**TikTok/trend angle:** "backyard glow-up" content is huge right now. Budget patio transformations, string light setups, Target outdoor section hauls.

### 2. FATHER'S DAY (June 21, 2026)
**Publish window:** June 8–21
**What performs best:** Father's Day spending is ~$20-25B (vs $35B Mother's Day) but gift guide traffic is strong. Under-$50 guides crush it.

**Top converting product categories:**
- Grilling tools & accessories
- Bluetooth speakers (JBL, Anker)
- Bluetooth trackers (Tile, AirTag)
- Noise-canceling earbuds
- Travel backpacks/dopp kits
- Grooming kits
- Funny/sentimental fill-in books
- Coolers & tumblers (Stanley, YETI dupes)

**Blog post ideas (niche it down like Mother's Day):**
- "father's day gifts for the grill dad" — spatula sets, meat thermometer, grill light, apron
- "father's day gifts for the outdoorsy dad" — cooler, headlamp, camp chair, pocket knife
- "father's day gifts for the tech dad" — AirTag, wireless charger, earbuds, phone mount
- "father's day gifts for the new dad" — diaper bag backpack, baby carrier, noise machine, funny onesie
- "father's day gifts under $15" — the budget hero post (highest click-through historically)
- "father's day gifts from the kids" — craft kits, coupons book, handprint frame, matching shirts

**TikTok/trend angle:** "things dad actually wants" format is viral. Anti-gift-guide (no ties, no mugs). Practical + funny. Also: "gifts for the dad who says he doesn't want anything."

### 5. BACK TO SCHOOL (August 2026)
**Publish window:** Start posting late July. Target & Walmart go early.

**Top categories:**

- Backpacks & lunch boxes
- Kids clothing basics
- Tech: headphones/earbuds, tablets, laptops (Apple dominates)

**Blog post ideas:**

- "the BTS tech guide" — earbuds, calculator, tablet, USB drive — what they actually need by grade
- "dorm room essentials under $25" — bedding, storage, desk accessories

**TikTok/trend angle:** "packing my kid's lunch" ASMR is huge. School supply hauls. "What I got my kids for back to school" format. Budget-shaming is out — finding deals is the flex.

## Content Strategy Notes

**From the research:**
1. **Niche down gift guides** — your Mother's Day approach (10+ sub-guides by personality type) is exactly what top blogs do. Replicate for Father's Day.
2. **Evergreen URLs** — use `/posts/fathers-day-gifts.html` not `/posts/fathers-day-gifts-2026.html`. Update annually. Better SEO.
3. **Publish 2-3 weeks early** — SEO needs crawl time. Pinterest needs 2-4 weeks to index. Gift guide searches start early.
4. **Price-tiered posts convert best** — "under $15 / under $25 / under $50" format is proven across every major deal blog.
5. **Multi-retailer always** — Target + Walmart + Old Navy + Kohl's + Amazon. Not just one store.
6. **Coupon/code callouts** — 80% of shoppers search for codes before checkout. If Mavely has codes, highlight them.
7. **Summer traffic dips** — expect ~20% lower engagement June-July. Counter with time-sensitive sale content and viral product tie-ins.
8. **Christmas in July** — underused but high-converting for deal blogs. "Get ahead" messaging works.
